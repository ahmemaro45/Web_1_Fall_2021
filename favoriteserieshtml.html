Plot Summary

Fran Fine starts off working in a bridal shop in Flushing, Queens. Her longtime boyfriend fires her and ends up breaking up with her, she ends up applying for the nanny position in the Sheffield residence. She gets to work their and learns to get accustomed to the big disparities between her and the upper class and the challenges she needs to face to accommodate Mr. Sheffield's kids.

Release Date

The first episode aired on November 3, 1993. The show has lasted for a total of 6 seasons and the last episode has aired on June 23, 1999.
